# Direction Papers

Three strand-specific mini lit reviews that complement the [AGI white paper](../agi-white-paper.md). Each is a focused, citation-rich essay that defends a thesis, surveys the landscape, and proposes a roadmap.

## Documents

- [`science-direction.md`](./science-direction.md), the new-science thesis. Argues that rich annotations for naturalistic stimuli are the gating variable for the next paradigm shift in cognitive-neuroscience analytics. Addresses [issue #1](https://github.com/Annotation-Garden/management/issues/1).
- [`tools-direction.md`](./tools-direction.md), the FAIR-services survey and the AGI adapter-layer commitment. Walks through what existing infrastructure already does, names the missing middleware, and scopes AGI's contribution. Addresses [issue #2](https://github.com/Annotation-Garden/management/issues/2).
- [`data-direction.md`](./data-direction.md), the dataset-coverage roadmap. Phases AGI's stimulus repositories from current flagships through second-wave and into the cognitive-task wedge.

## Reading order

For a new contributor or grant reviewer who has read the white paper:

1. [`science-direction.md`](./science-direction.md) first. Establishes why annotation infrastructure matters scientifically, not just operationally.
2. [`data-direction.md`](./data-direction.md) next. Names the datasets where the science applies and where AGI invests.
3. [`tools-direction.md`](./tools-direction.md) last. Explains how the infrastructure is built and which existing services it composes with.

## Source materials

Every claim cites the corresponding Phase 1 paper-card by relative path, e.g. [`../research/collection/science/huth2016-semantic-maps/card.md`](../research/collection/science/huth2016-semantic-maps/card.md). Each doc closes with a flat references section keyed to BibTeX in [`tools.bib`](../research/collection/tools/tools.bib), [`data.bib`](../research/collection/data/data.bib), and [`science.bib`](../research/collection/science/science.bib) respectively.

The Phase 2 syntheses ([tool-ontology](../research/synthesis/tool-ontology.md), [dataset-hierarchy](../research/synthesis/dataset-hierarchy.md), [science-map](../research/synthesis/science-map.md), [gap-analysis](../research/synthesis/gap-analysis.md), [scope-diagram](../research/synthesis/scope-diagram.md)) are the inventories these direction papers draw on.

## Style

- Abbreviations defined on first use within each doc.
- No em-dashes; commas or semicolons.
- No emojis.
- Mini lit reviews, not manifestos: the voice is descriptive and synthesis-driven, not exhortatory.
